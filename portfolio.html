Baccalaureate Achievement

In the year 2020, I proudly earned my Baccalaureate degree, marking a significant milestone in my academic journey. This achievement not only symbolized the culmination of years of hard work and dedication but also laid the foundation for my future pursuit of knowledge and success.

University Enrollent

In 2021, I embarked on a new chapter of my life by joining university to pursue a degree in computer science. This decision was driven by my passion for technology and my desire to delve deeper into the world of programming, software development, and the vast realm of computer science.

Internship at Tunisie Telecom

During the year 2022, I had the privilege of working as an intern at Tunisie Telecom in Zaghouan, Tunisia. This experience provided me with invaluable insights into the fundamental structure of telecommunications networks. It was a stepping stone that allowed me to grasp the intricate workings of network infrastructure, which is foundational in the field of computer science.

Developer Internship at Almindhar

In 2023, I took on an exciting opportunity as a developer intern at Almindhar. During this period, I honed my skills in web development, specifically focusing on WordPress. I was tasked with the responsibility of maintaining an existing website and actively contributing to the development of a brand new website. This experience allowed me to apply my knowledge to real-world projects and further refine my expertise in web development.
